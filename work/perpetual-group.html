§ Perpetual Group · Digital marketing agency

First clients for a new agency.

01 /The starting point

Feodor Kozmin was standing up Perpetual Group — a new digital marketing practice. He had a clear idea of what he wanted to sell, a few people he could serve well, and zero outbound motion.

That's the hardest position to start from. Not "our outbound is broken." Just "there is no outbound yet, and I don't know where to begin without burning the first ninety days on the wrong approach."

02 /What we did

Three calls before anything was sent:

  • Defined who Perpetual could genuinely help at this stage — not aspirational buyers, but the ones where the offer made clear sense today.
  • Wrote the outreach to sound like a founder reaching out, because a founder was. No agency voice. No hedging.
  • Built sending infrastructure that could grow with the business — warmed properly, one domain at a time, so the first six months didn't cost him the next twelve.

Then we ran it. LinkedIn and email, daily, with every reply handled the same day and every learning fed back into the targeting the next week.

03 /What changed

The conversations started inside two weeks. The first clients came through the door in the first couple of months. More importantly, Feodor had something he could run himself from that point on — a playbook he'd seen work on his own business, not a deck he'd bought.

The point of doing it this way was always to set up a function, not to run a campaign. When the engagement ended, Perpetual had a pipeline motion that kept producing.

04 /Why this worked

Founders starting from zero are the hardest buyers to serve well. They have no benchmarks, so "good" is hard to prove. They have no volume yet, so the work has to be hands-on in a way that doesn't scale.

What works is doing the work with them, not just for them. Sitting on the first calls. Reading the first replies. Adjusting in the same week, not the next quarter. That's structurally what Ascent runs — one operator, full ownership, end to end. The agency-founder dynamic is a specific case. The structure underneath it is general.
